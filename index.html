About Me

Hi, I'm Colin — a second-year Data Science and Mathematics dual major at Northeastern University, set to graduate in May 2028. I grew up on Long Island, New York, and I speak English, Cantonese, and Spanish. My academic and professional interests sit at the intersection of data science, mathematics, and quantitative finance, and I am always looking for new ways to apply rigorous analytical thinking to real-world problems.

At Northeastern, I have built a strong foundation in statistics, machine learning, and software engineering. Some of my favorite coursework includes information visualization, nonlinear dynamics, and abstract algebra. I enjoy projects that blend technical depth with practical impact — whether that means training machine-learning models on tens of thousands of loan records, designing data pipelines, or building full-stack tools that surface market insights in real time.

Outside of academics, I stay active as a youth tennis coach at Cunningham Tennis and volunteer as a teacher's aide working with individuals with developmental differences. I also have a strong personal interest in day trading and the stock market, which drives much of the project work you will find on this site.
